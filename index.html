How we do it

Choose how you plug in.

Every program is curated and application-based. Start online, come to the Valley when you're ready.

OnlineWeekly

Founder Circle

Your weekly rhythm with Silicon Valley. Small pods of founders meet active US investors every two weeks — pitch, get direct feedback, sharpen your raise. Off-the-record sessions with founders who recently closed $1M+ rounds, plus an accountability pair that keeps you moving.

In personOne weekSF & Bay Area

Silicon Valley Bootcamp

Seven days inside the ecosystem: meetings at Stanford, Google, Meta, Berkeley, and top accelerators; private dinners with founders and VCs; and the kind of connections that usually take a year to build. Each edition is unique — new speakers, new rooms, same outcome: you leave with a Valley network.

OnlineBay Area

Sessions & Events

Invite-only roundtables with Silicon Valley heavyweights — like the operators behind Higgsfield's 0→$200M ARR run and YC-backed founders who've scaled to $65M ARR — plus private dinners, demo nights, and founder gatherings across SF and Palo Alto.

See upcoming events →

Cohort-basedOnlineBay Area

Acceleration Program

An application-only sprint to make your company fundable in the US: investor-ready narrative and deck, GTM built for the US market, hands-on work with mentors from our bench, and pitch sessions with active VC funds from our network. Cohorts open a few times a year.

Who it's for

A fit — or not a fit.

A fit

  • Mission-driven AI and deep-tech founders from anywhere in the world
  • Scaling — or committed to scaling — in the US market
  • Planning to raise in the next 3–12 months
  • Ready to show up in Silicon Valley at least a few times a year

Not a fit

  • Non-tech businesses
  • No clear goal — “just exploring”
  • Not ready to come to Silicon Valley
  • Looking for a quick intro, not a relationship
